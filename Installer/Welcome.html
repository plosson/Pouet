Pouet Audio Driver

This installer will add a Pouet virtual microphone to your Mac along with a companion app.

What gets installed:

  • Pouet driver — a virtual microphone input device
  • Pouet.app — GUI app for managing the proxy, playing sounds, and settings

After installation, open Pouet from your Applications folder and select a real microphone to proxy through the virtual device.

The driver runs entirely in user space. No kernel extension is installed.
